The logic behind the triage.

Every intake system is only as good as the rules behind it. Here's how I score incoming requests to ensure the right work gets the right attention at the right time.

P0 — Drop everything. Deadline within 48 hours AND high business impact. Immediate kickoff. Daily leadership updates. All hands.
P1 — This week. Within 1 week deadline OR cross-functional with medium/high impact. Kickoff within 24 hours. Weekly status updates.
P2 — Scheduled. 2+ week runway with defined impact. Enters the production queue. Milestone check-ins as needed.
